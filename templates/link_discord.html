How It Works

How to Find Your Discord ID

  1. Open Discord and go to User Settings
  2. Navigate to "Advanced" in the left sidebar
  3. Enable "Developer Mode" by toggling the switch
  4. Right-click on your username in any chat and select "Copy ID"
  5. Paste the ID in the field to the left
